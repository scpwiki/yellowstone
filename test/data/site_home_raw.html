FEATURED SCP

SCP-4386: B Gone, B

by CyrusFiredawn | Selected by Staff

As events occur in reality, they subsequently occur within the simulation following an average delay of 23 hours. SCP-4386-02 through 05 are unaware that their observed world is a simulation.
